OpenCV Lane Detection

During my final semester of college, I worked on an independent research project with my professor, Dr. Li. This project consisted of creating an application with OpenCV to detect driving lanes on the highway. The results can be seen to the right. The top left video is the original video recorded on an iPhone. The bottom left video is the original video converted to the HSV colorspace, which makes it easier to identify the driving lanes. The bottom right video is the original video with the canny edge finding algorithm applied to find all edges. With all of this data, the lanes can be identified and lines can be created and overlaid on the original video. This final video in the top left is the original video with the "found" lanes drawn overtop the video.
